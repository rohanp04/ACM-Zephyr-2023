Our Events

Here are the events for Zephyr 2023

Scavenger Hunt

Get ready for an exciting Bollywood Treasure Hunt challenge! In this thrilling group game, you'll put your clue-finding skills to the test, navigating through a series of tasks and Bollywood-related riddles to reach the grand finale.

WWE PS4

Step into the ring and unleash your inner superstar in WWE on PS4! Get ready for heart-pounding action, iconic rivalries, and electrifying gameplay. It's time to become the champion of the WWE universe!

Coding Maniacs

Coding Maniacs is the perfect pace-based competition to improve your coding skills and test our limits. Participants have to write code solutions to multiple problem statements with real life constraints, like time complexity, space complexity along with fix input output format.

Debug the Code

The Coding Contest is based on C/C++ Programming. The questions must be solved using these languages. Students from any discipline may participate and it is an individual task. It will have three stages. Codes will be evaluated on the basis of correctness of code, time complexity and space complexity.

Ethical Hacking Workshop

Prepare for an immersive Ethical Hacking Workshop experience. Join us on a journey where you'll unravel the secrets of cybersecurity, working in groups to tackle tasks and clues, all designed to enhance your proficiency in ethical hacking.

Neon Bowling

Step into a neon wonderland with Neon Bowling! Gather your friends and prepare for a dazzling adventure, where you'll roll, strike, and have a glowing good time in this electrifying group bowling game.

Powershow

Welcome to PowerShow, an extraordinary presentation experience! Get ready to showcase your ideas with flair and impact. In this dynamic event, you'll captivate your audience and harness the power of compelling presentations.

Counter Strike

Gear up for intense combat in the world of Counter-Strike! Join forces with your team, strategize, and engage in high-stakes missions. In this thrilling game, your skills and tactics will be put to the ultimate test.

Escape room

An escape room, also known as an escape game, puzzle room, or exit game, is a game in which a team of players discover clues, solve puzzles, and accomplish tasks in one or more rooms in order to accomplish a specific goal in a limited amount of time. The goal is often to escape from the site of the game.

ZEPHYR 2023

Ever since it was first organized in 2004, Zephyr has been the most awaited event among the students of Thakur College of Engineering and Technology. This technical festival aims at providing students with platform to enhance their technical skills. Each year with a new edition, Zephyr brings new events such as workshops on in-demand technology and skills along with gaming tournaments and fun events. This 3-day fest has been recoding and increasing footfall of Students, Teachers and Public in general. Join in Zephyr 2023 to get a glimpse of the best of TCET.
